Alexander Cristobal

It ticks, it tocks, faster than a bullet.

It thumps, it beats, dreading every second.

An endless winter pours inside me;

A rain of hail, so cold, so fierce,

Stirring my fears in a dark abyss.

“Who to talk to, who to laugh with”,

These thoughts and worries in my head,

Until out of the blue, a light shines in the darkness


It ticks, it tocks, slower than snail

It thumps, it beats, cherishing every second.

My worries and doubts vanish in an instant,

In a group so accepting, so comforting and kind.

Our friendship, a symphony of hearts.

No longer just six bodies, but one soul.

Mere words cannot describe such beauty and passion;

That is our love that will last for all eternity.
